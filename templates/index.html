How to use this site:

There are 3 main forms of functionality:


/plot_3d_contour/int:bound_min/int:bound_max/path:equation

In this case, bound_min and bound_max represent the minimum and maximum x and y values.

Example input: /plot_3d_contour/-5/5/3x^2+2y^3


/plot_3d_surface/int:bound_min/int:bound_max/path:equation
Similarly to the previous one, bound_min and bound_max represent the minimum and maximum x and y values.

Example input: /plot_3d_surface/-5/5/1x^4+3y^1


/plot_kde/string:color/path:data

Example input: /plot_kde/blue/1,2,6,3,4,2,6,3,4,3,2,-5,-2
